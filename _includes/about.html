Who We Are

Impact++ is a student organization located at UW Seattle. We connect CS students with projects which benefit society as a whole. Our partners include Microsoft Philanthropies’ Hack For Good team, the King County Sheriff’s Office Foundation, the Grace Children’s Foundation, and Farestart. Our club is primarily focused on team based projects, which are provided by our affiliates. Teams of 5-7 student developers then work together throughout the year to tackle these problems, meeting once a week. Another significant portion of our contribution to the open source world comes from our annual hackathon.

Our Vision

A collective of students who believe in using their skills to advance the intersection of technology and social good.
