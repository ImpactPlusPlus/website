About

Impact++ is a student organization located at UW Seattle. We connect CS students with projects which benefit society as a whole. Our partners include Microsoft Philanthropies’ Hack For Good team, the King County Sheriff’s Office Foundation, the Grace Children’s Foundation, and Farestart.
